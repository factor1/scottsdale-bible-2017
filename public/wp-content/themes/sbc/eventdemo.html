College

Going off to college poses many challenges for our young adults. For some, they are on their own for the first time, asking questions about what really matters in life and God’s ultimate plan for them.

The College Group seeks to meet the needs of these students who are in a very transitional time through a mix of contemporary worship and teaching, small groups, fun activities, mission trips and individual accountability. Great relationships are built here!

We invite you to visit us at our weekly meeting, Tuesday evenings at 7:30. We meet at the Cactus Campus - 2501 East Cactus Road, Phoenix, AZ 85032

Following our bible study, some of the guys head back to Scottsdale Bible for a few hours of basketball.

If you would like more information about our ministry, please contact Carol Hubner, 480.824.7280 or chubner@sbcaz.org.
